<p>Rising the security and reliability level of development and deployment of e-Systems taking in consideration the risks of cyber-attacks and specific issues.</p>
<p>The education of high level specialists in IT&amp;C and Informatics Security.</p>
<p>To share and transfer the practical &amp; theoretical knowledge from IT&amp;C Security field among the developers, administrators, project managers and users.
